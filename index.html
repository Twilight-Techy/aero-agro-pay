The Core Loop

From Payment to Airborne in Minutes

01
📱

Farmer Initiates Payment

Via the OPay mobile app or a USSD shortcode on any feature phone. The farmer selects mission type: spraying or crop-health mapping.

OPay App → AeroAgro Pay or *955*3*1*SPRAYING#
02
💳

OPay Micro-Payment

A mission fee (from ₦2,500) is instantly debited from the farmer's OPay wallet or collected via the nearest POS agent.

100% within OPay ecosystem
03
🚁

Drone Autonomously Deploys

The payment webhook triggers the nearest Aero-Agent's drone. Motors arm. Flight path is computed. Drone takes off.

≈ 90 seconds to airborne
04
🌾

Mission Complete. Crops Protected.

Precise pesticide delivery or multispectral imaging. Farmer receives SMS report. Mission logged to OPay dashboard.

Data-driven yield intelligence
